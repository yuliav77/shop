Speaking

The very first advice - speak, speak, speak!

There are several hints to improve your speaking skills:

  1. Find An English-Speaking Conversation Partner

    First of all, it's really important to find native speakers to practice with. You can meet a local conversation partner or participate in foreign-language forums on the internet.

    Another good way - to attend English communication clubs.

  2. Learn both single words and phrases. Widen your active vocabulary

    While both watching movies or reading books just write down new interesting words and phrases in special notebook. Browse it every day and use new words. So you can extend your active vocabulary.

  3. Record your voice

    Hearing yourself on tape shows you mistakes you might not realize.
