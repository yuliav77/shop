Reading

The very first advice - read a loads of English content: articles, blogs, books. Read every single day!

Below you will find a couple of interesting books, which are not very difficult for reading:

"Twenties Girl"
Sophie Kinsella

It’s a story of an unlikely friendship, a story of fashion, love, dancing, and some cringingly embarrassing moments!

"Can You Keep a Secret?"
Sophie Kinsella

What if a stranger knew all your embarrassing secrets? What if that stranger turned out to be your boss?

"The Giver"
Lois Lowry

It is set in a society which at first appears to be utopian but is revealed to be dystopian as the story progresses.

"Divergent"
Veronica Roth

The novel Divergent features a post-apocalyptic version of Chicago and follows Beatrice "Tris" Prior as she explores her identity within a society.
