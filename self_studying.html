About self-learning

There are a great deal of learning resourses online! You can choose anything and study how you want, where you want and when you want. Everything is up to you!

Sometimes self-learning might be quite useful, however it doesn't suit everybody.

Advantages

  1. It's cheap or even free
  2. You can choose your own pace, convinient location and time
  3. You are less stressed

Disadvantages

  1. Slow evolution
  2. Lack of face-to-face communication
  3. You need self-discipline
